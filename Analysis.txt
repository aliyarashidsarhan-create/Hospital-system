Analysis Phase Overview

The Analysis Phase is the stage in the software development lifecycle (SDLC) where the requirements of the system are thoroughly studied and documented. Its primary purpose is to understand what the hospital system must do before designing or coding begins.

Importance:

Ensures the software meets hospital needs (patients, staff, admin).

Reduces risks of errors, rework, and scope creep.

Serves as a blueprint for design and development.
